# 🛒 Tindera Pro

**A modern Point of Sale system built for sari-sari stores and small businesses in the Philippines.**

Tindera Pro helps store owners manage products, inventory, and sales through a simple, fast, and reliable mobile experience.

> 🚧 Tindera Pro is currently under active development.

---

## Features

### Authentication
- Google Sign-In
- Facebook Login
- Passwordless Authentication

### Point of Sale
- Fast checkout
- Shopping cart
- Barcode scanning *(Coming Soon)*
- Cash and digital payments
- Digital receipts

### Inventory
- Product management
- Categories
- Stock monitoring
- Low stock alerts

### Sales & Reports
- Daily, weekly, and monthly reports
- Best-selling products
- Revenue insights

### Store Management
- Store profile
- Receipt customization
- Employee management *(Planned)*
